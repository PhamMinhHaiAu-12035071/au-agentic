## Issues vs Discussions

**Open an issue** for:
- Bug reports
- Feature requests
- Documentation errors

**Start a discussion** for:
- Questions about usage
- Ideas and feedback
- Show and tell

## Response Time

This is maintained by volunteers. Expect:
- Bugs: 2-3 days
- Features: 1-2 weeks
- Questions: 1-2 days
