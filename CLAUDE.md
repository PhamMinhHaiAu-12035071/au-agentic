# Claude-Specific Compaction Rules

When working in compact mode, always preserve these in your report:

- **Docs consulted:** List files read from docs/ai/ or docs/
- **Files edited:** Which files you modified
- **Commands run:** Output from verification commands
- **Assumptions made:** What you inferred and why
- **Risks identified:** Known gaps or uncertainties

# Delegation Note

For repetitive workflows, prefer skills/hooks over inline implementation.
